# Conv Decisions - 2026-05-26

## 1. Forward-migrate off /old rather than repoint tests/redirects to it
**Type:** Strategic
**Topics:** astro, cc-workflow, testing

**Trigger:** Discovery that /old holds the working flows and root holds Matt stubs; no redirect layer (user's explicit choice).

**Options Considered:**
1. Fix middleware/hrefs to point at /old; repoint 30 e2e specs to /old
2. Forward-migrate: wire minimal root app, stub nav, convert /old pages one at a time ← Chosen

**Decision:** Treat /old as a conversion source; build the new root app incrementally per the user's 4-phase plan.

**Rationale:** /old is being dismantled; stabilizing it (or its tests) is throwaway work. e2e follows the new routes.

**Consequences:** [E2E-OLD] re-scoped to [E2E-MIG]; RTMIG-1..4 block created.
