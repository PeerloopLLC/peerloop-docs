## 1. `/feeds` = Discover destination (Option A); FeedsHub composite → `/` landing
**Type:** Strategic
**Topics:** astro, docs-infra

**Trigger:** Porting the Discover "Feeds" destination collided with the pre-existing unmounted `FeedsHub` composite at the same `/feeds` path.

**Options Considered:**
1. Port the discover-feeds destination to `/feeds` ← Chosen
2. Port the FeedsHub composite to `/feeds`
3. Merge both into one `/feeds`

**Decision:** `/feeds` becomes the Matt discover destination (discovery grid plus role-aware Your-Feeds directory), mirroring `/communities` and `/members`. The `FeedsHub` composite stays out and is destined for the `/` landing page per Matt.

**Rationale:** Matt wants FeedsHub as the `/` landing (visitor plus personalized-logged-in); `/` will accrete more client content; keeping `/feeds` composite-free makes it a clean sibling; no orphaned discovery grid.

**Consequences:** Built 4 new components plus page; corrected `project_feeds_hub.md`; created `[HOME-FEEDSHUB]` (#35) for the `/` work.
