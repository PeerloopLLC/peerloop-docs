**User:** Reported Guy Rymberg appearing 4 times on `/discover/teachers` with different student counts (67, 52, 38, 15). Shared screenshot.

### Understanding st.id vs user_id

**User:** Asked what `st.id` actually does.

**Claude:** Explained that `st.id` is the primary key of `student_teachers` — a per-course certification record ID. It answers "which teaching assignment?" while `user_id` answers "which teacher?" Directory pages care about the teacher, not the assignment.
