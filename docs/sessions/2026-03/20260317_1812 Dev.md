### Client Request: Book Now Feature

**User:** "The client has asked for the ability of a student and a teacher to book a session to start just as soon as the student has paid (if its the first module) or to change book a sessin right away rather than pick a time slot. It is imprtant that you cannot book an extra session: a 2 session course would not get a thiird session. A completed 2 module course would not get a thirsd session either. Raised key questions: teacher awareness, unilateral vs coordinated, who can initiate.
