## 1. RG-PUBLIC — keep fully deferred until the marketing redesign
**Type:** Strategic
**Topics:** route-migration, matt-design

**Trigger:** User picked the RG-PUBLIC disposition discussion after RG-ADMIN closed; investigation showed 14 marketing pages still `/old` with root paths 404 and the app-wide footer linking to them.

**Options Considered:**
1. Partial — port the stable legal/support pages (privacy/terms/cookies/help/contact/faq), defer pure-marketing
2. Full sweep — port all 14 now (closes RTMIG-4)
3. Keep deferred, fix footer links only (repoint to `/old` or accept 404)

**Decision:** Keep RG-PUBLIC fully deferred; do NOT port or repoint; accept the app-wide footer root-link 404s as intentional-pending-redesign.

**Rationale:** The marketing pages are redesign-likely (deferred Conv 331); porting them to Matt now risks being thrown away. The footer-404 is a known, accepted consequence — not a bug to patch.

**Consequences:** RTMIG-4 umbrella is at its planned stopping point (only RG-PUBLIC remains, now explicitly parked). Recorded in route-migration README + task #3 so a future conv won't re-raise the footer-404s.
