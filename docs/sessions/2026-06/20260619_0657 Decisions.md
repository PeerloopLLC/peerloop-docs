## 1. Mint `text-body-medium-medium` (16/500) for the form-label gap
**Type:** Strategic
**Topics:** typography, matt-design

**Trigger:** NotificationSettings labels are 16px medium (`font-medium`, no size); §09 has 16/400 + 16/600 but no 16/500. Recurs across all 4 settings islands.

**Options Considered:**
1. Mint `--body-medium-medium` 16/500 (body regime) ← Chosen
2. Bump labels to `text-body-medium-bold` (16/600)
3. Drop labels to `text-body-default-medium` (14/500)

**Decision:** Mint the body-regime 16/500 token in `tokens-typography.css` + bridge, marked CC-minted (NOT @matt-source).

**Rationale:** Only zero-visual-change option; faithful to the legacy 16/500; the gap is real and recurring; follows the established CC-mint precedent (`--body-default-bold`, `--body-small-bold` Conv 300; `--body-default-prose` Conv 298). Per §Solution Quality, durable + faithful over a quick weight/size shim.

**Consequences:** New design-system token documented in §09 (§9.2b) + role-discipline table; applies to the remaining 3 settings islands' labels.
