**Topics:** astro, ssr, matt-design

**Trigger:** [JOURNEY-LOOP] scope step 1 literally said "emit gates {enrolled,paid,certified} + sessions {completed,scheduled,total,…}".

**Options Considered:**

**Consequences:** No loader change this conv; smaller blast radius; documented as a deliberate deviation in `plan/enroll-nav/README.md`.
