## 3. indigo → brand by tonal order (sparse-ramp mapping)
**Type:** Implementation
**Topics:** conformance, palette

**Trigger:** PromoteButton's indigo-50/200/600/700 palette needed mapping; the brand ramp is sparse {100,300,500} (and brand IS the indigo/violet family — brand-300 = #584DF4).

**Decision:** Map by tonal order — slate-500→neutral-500; hover indigo-600→brand-500 (text contrast), indigo-50→brand-100, indigo-200→brand-300; HOT bg indigo-600→brand-300 + hover indigo-700→brand-500 (preserves the darken) + border→brand-300; green-600→success-500.

**Rationale:** Follows the established AdminPagination indigo→brand precedent; the sparse ramp can't absorb 4 indigo steps 1:1, so tonal-order with text-vs-fill contrast judgment.

**Consequences:** PromoteButton fully tokenized; no brand-700 invented (would have been inert).
