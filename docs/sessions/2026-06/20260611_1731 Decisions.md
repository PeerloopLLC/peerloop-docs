# Conv Decisions - 2026-06-11

## 1. Destination-level intent, not action-level auto-perform
**Type:** Implementation
**Topics:** smart-feed, signup, auth

**Trigger:** The HOME-FEED-MERGE Phase 6 design says "sign up, then join X / take Y" — ambiguous between "return them to X" (destination) and "auto-join/enroll after signup" (action).

**Options Considered:**
1. Destination-level — visitor CTA → `/signup?redirect=<entity>`; after signup land on the entity page with the now-authed action button ← Chosen
2. Action-level — encode `&action=join|enroll` and auto-perform post-signup

**Decision:** Destination-level only.

**Rationale:** Matches the design's core phrasing ("return them to X") AND the established `EnrollButton` pattern (so not novel). Action-level is asymmetric and risky — course enroll routes through Stripe checkout, and auto-redirecting a fresh signup to a payment page is hostile. The authed "one-click join/enroll from discovery" is itself a future enhancement.

**Consequences:** CTA label unchanged for visitors (expresses intent; honesty from the "from X · not joined" badge). No client component edit. Action-level noted as deferred.
