- Earn money while learning
- Flexible schedule

### How to Become an S-T

1. **Complete a Course**
   - Enroll in a course
   - Finish all modules
   - Pass any assessments

2. **Get Recommended**
   - Your S-T reviews your work
   - Creator certifies you
   - Usually 1-2 weeks

3. **Start Teaching**
   - Set your availability
   - Get matched with students
   - Conduct sessions

4. **Earn & Grow**
   - 70% of session fees
   - Build your reputation
   - Teach multiple courses
