### How Pricing Works

**For Students:**
- Course enrollment fee (one-time)
- Includes all content + sessions
- No subscription, no hidden fees

**Session Fees:**
- Price per live session
- Varies by course/duration
- Paid at booking

### Price Breakdown Visual
- Course fee: Creator sets
- Session fee: Creator sets
- Platform fee: 15%
- S-T earnings: 70%
- Creator royalty: 15%
